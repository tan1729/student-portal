About Us Page

ORCA7 is the educational students portal, a vital component of the School Management Software.Students may check assignments, download material, see grade results, and access many more functions via the student portal. Choosing a software gateway for college might be challenging with many possibilities. That’s why we’ve set up a guide to assist you in reducing your quest and choosing the right system. The constant updating of the student's attendance by the professors is the first and most significant feature of the site. After each lesson, teachers may apply their portal login to record student and class attendance. The site also displays critical information like student weeks, sports weeks, and other events and activities for students to observe. The exam and the mid-exams timings and the details are also shared there.
